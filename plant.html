Sunflower

Helianthus annuus ¬∑ Asteraceae (Daisy Family) ¬∑ Common Sunflower ¬∑ Giant Sunflower

Quick Snapshot
Type: Annual Flowering Herb Origin: Native to North America Season: Summer to Early Fall Interesting fact: Young sunflowers follow the sun across the sky, a movement called heliotropism.
Description: Sunflowers are tall, bright yellow flowering plants known for their large sun shaped blooms and edible seeds. Their faces track sunlight when young, helping maximize growth and energy production.

Roasted Sunflower Seeds

Crunchy, nutty roasted seeds that make a healthy snack or salad topping.

Ingredients
  • Raw sunflower seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Salt or seasoning of choice
Steps
  1. Rinse and dry sunflower seeds.
  2. Toss with oil and seasoning.
  3. Roast at 325¬∞F for 25 to 30 minutes until golden.

Tip: Store in an airtight container. Add to salads, granola, or trail mix.
